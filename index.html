MORE INFO ON MILES MORALES

Miles Morales appears in the Ultimate Spider-Man animated series, voiced by Donald Glover (in "The Spider-Verse") and subsequently by Ogie Banks. After being alluded to in the first season, Miles is introduced in the third season four-part episode "The Spider-Verse". Miles returns as a recurring character in the fourth season, during which he gets stranded in Peter Parker's universe, joins the Web Warriors, and takes on the alias Kid Arachnid. Miles Morales appears in the Marvel Super Hero Adventures animated series, voiced by Zac Siewert. Miles Morales appears in the Spider-Man animated series, voiced by Nadji Jeter. He is introduced as a normal teenager but obtains his abilities in the season-one episode "Ultimate Spider-Man", after which Peter Parker mentors him. The Miles Morales incarnation of Spider-Man will appear in Spidey and His Amazing Friends, which will premiere on Disney Junior in 2021.

BEST FIGHTING GAMES


Teen Titans Battlegrounds

Teen Titans Battleground allows you to play as one of your favorite titans and face off against other players. You can then collect coins to unlock new waves and new characters that will make things even more interesting. Each character has a range of abilities and it is up to you to find out which one suits your own playstyle.


Counter Blox

If you are a fan of games like Call of Duty or Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, then you will like the setup of this game. It puts you into five versus five matches, and you can earn money by eliminating opponents and playing the objective. You can run around with guns or knives to take out your opponents and there are plenty of options to customize your character.


Ninja Legends

Ninja Legends is a pretty neat fighting game that features agile movements, Ninjitsu, and lots and lots of combat. The game is notable for having over 1 billion visits despite having been released less than two years ago. The game has a number of upgradable options, too, including permanent upgrades to pets and capacity and even unlocking a new location.


The Mirror

This is the only single-player horror game included in the list, and it doesn't disappoint. The Mirror will certainly leave a mark on any player, as it is truly an unforgettable horror experience. Full of creepy sounds and grotesque images, players have to solve an obstacle course by looking at only their own reflection and finding clues in the mirror. For those who already have a tough relationship with the sometimes creepy nature of mirrors, this game will definitely keep them on the edge of their seats.
